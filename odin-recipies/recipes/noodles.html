Noodles

Description

Cabbage and noodles, cooked with bacon, is comfort food supreme, great for a chilly day.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8oz) package egg noodles
  • 3 tbl spoon butter
  • 1/2lbs bacon
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 small cabbage (chopped)
  • Dash garlic salt

Directions

  1. Boil egg noodles until firm (about 5 minutes). Drain, return to pot and add butter.
  2. Cook bacon is large, deep skillet (medium-high heat, about 10 minutes). Drain.
  3. Place onion on skillet with bacon grease, cook and stir (medium heat). Chop bacon, add, and combine with noodles
