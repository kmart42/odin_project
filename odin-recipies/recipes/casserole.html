John Wayne Casserole

Description

Made this on a lark and it ended up being one of the best one-pan meals I've discovered.

Ingredients

  • 16oz can refrigerated biscuits
  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 1oz taco seasoning
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 medium onion (halved and sliced)
  • 1 medium red bell pepper (havled and sliced)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 8oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 medium tomatoes (sliced)
  • 4oz jar sliced jalapeño peppers

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and bake biscuit dough for 15-20 min
  2. Cook ground beef (5 min), drain fat, stir in taco seasoning. Add onion and bell peppers (5 more min)
  3. Combine sour cream, mayo, cheddar, onion in bowl. Layer in browned meat and remaining veggies
  4. Bake uncovered at 350°F 30-40 minutes
